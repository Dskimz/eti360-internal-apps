- **Duke of Ed**
The Duke of Edinburgh’s International Award (Duke of Ed) challenges students in Grades 9 to 12 to foster positive lifestyle habits and skills through physical activities, outdoor expeditions and service to the community. The award has three levels: Bronze, Silver and Gold. Each level requires a higher degree of commitment to complete. At any time, more than 100 BSS students are working on levels for the Duke of Ed.
