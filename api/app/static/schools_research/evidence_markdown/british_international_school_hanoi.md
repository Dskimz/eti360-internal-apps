## NORD ANGLIA EXPEDITIONS IN SWITZERLAND
Dazzling blue skies, towering peaks, and jaw-dropping vistas – Switzerland is a truly spectacular country. It’s the perfect place to explore or embark on an exhilarating alpine adventure.
- For students aged 12+
Our Experience Switzerland Expedition offers something for everyone, bringing students together from around the world to discover all the country has to offer. Your child will enjoy challenging but accessible treks, such as the Tour de I’Argentine, and visit Glacier 3000 to learn about glacial retreat. They’ll also wander the lanes and tracks of idyllic villages, including Gruyère, and enjoy a behind-the-scenes look at how Swiss chocolate and cheese are made.
Trek Switzerland
- For students aged 13+
From our expedition base in Les Plans sur Bex, students embark on a three-day, two-night trek through the Swiss Alps. The area’s unique terrain features high passes and the chance to traverse alpine glaciers, which offer amazing views of Mont Blanc. Staying in local mountain huts and camps, our trekkers take the lead, as they learn about navigation, self-care, teamwork, cooking, and decision-making. We also offer an Advanced Trekking Expedition.
Snow Sports
- For students aged 12+
Our week-long Snow Sports Expedition gives students of all abilities the chance to ski, snowboard, and snowshoe through breathtaking alpine scenery. Based in Les Plan sur Bex, which is a short journey to the picturesque snow sports town of Villars-sur-Ollon, the trip gives winter sports enthusiasts the chance to develop their skills in their chosen discipline, divided into groups based on experience.
