- ## How Do ISKL Students Get to School?
ISKL’s campus is located 3 km from Kuala Lumpur City Centre (KLCC) and is accessible via a dedicated and efficient school bus service, supporting the travel needs of over 50% of students.
All buses are strictly controlled for safety, and every route is monitored by ride-along bus assistants who ensure that students are dropped off at the designated location.
- ### Did you know?
Around 55% of students travel to school by bus, and of those students:
* 33% travel from Jalan U-Thant, Jalan Ampang, and Ampang Hilir
- Around 55% of students travel to school by bus, and of those students:
