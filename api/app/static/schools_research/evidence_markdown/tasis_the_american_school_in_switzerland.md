After learning about the history of Nepal and the plight of Tibetan refugees, TASIS students, with the help and insights of the Tserok community, raise funds and travel to Nepal _for two weeks during Spring Break_ to help develop infrastructure for the Tserok Tibetan refugee camp. - ## Kenya: WISER
WISER is a girls-only boarding secondary school in Muhuru Bay, Kenya, a struggling rural community on Lake Victoria. After examining issues of universal education and gender equality, students spend _February Academic Travel_ getting to know the community of Muhuru Bay, Kenya. They assist at local primary schools, educate the community on the benefits of purified water, visit local medical clinics, and work closely with the WISER school.
